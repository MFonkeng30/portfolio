About Me


Hi, I'm Muendem Fonkeng. I live in Lebanon, TN. My current occupation is a Laundry Attendent at the Hampton Inn located in Mount Juliet. I grew up in two different places, Kent, OH til I was 10 and then Birmingham, AL. I moved to Tennessee about 5 years ago and lived in Mount Juliet for 3 years. I currently live in Lebanon. I got started with web development coding through a coding boot camp here at Vanderbilt University. Growing up, I had an interest in computers and knew that I wanted to do something in that field. I thought about college for computer engineering and even attempted going to one at one point, but as the years passed by, the idea of computer engineering came to a halt. Worked different jobs over the years, but wasn't really satisfied. I felt like I was missing something. One day, I saw a post online that caught my attention and been learning to code ever since.
